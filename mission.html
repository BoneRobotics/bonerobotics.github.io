Mission
We build the backbone of the Physical AI era.

Autonomous systems are reshaping national security and public safety. The hard problem isn't the algorithm — it's the infrastructure that makes intelligent machines real at scale.

We founded Bone Robotics to solve exactly that: the full-stack physical AI supply chain, from silicon to sortie.

Korea built the industrial foundations of the modern world — steel, semiconductors, shipbuilding. We are building its next.

Allied Manufacturing Sovereignty
Relocating critical manufacturing to allied ground — making Korea the trusted production hub for the Physical AI era, for Korea and its allies. Not Made in China. Made in Korea.
End-to-End Ownership
From component sourcing and manufacturing to software and field operations — we control the full stack. A platform company building durable industrial infrastructure. Not a services company. Not an integrator.
Built to Last
We are building the kind of company Korea has produced before — Hyundai, POSCO, Samsung — enterprises that defined an era of industry. The Physical AI era needs its own legacy company. We are building it.
