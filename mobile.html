Why we exist

A nation is only as resilient as the infrastructure beneath it.

Most robotics companies build a product. Most AI companies build a model. Most defense primes integrate other people's parts.

Bone owns and integrates every layer — hardware, autonomy, sensors, comms, manufacturing, and command-and-control — into one capability that behaves as a whole. In the field. Under contract. When conditions turn hostile.

We start where the need is most immediate and the stakes are highest: securing the ports, shipyards, coastal bases, and energy facilities that allied nations cannot afford to lose.

Team · Why we started

We build for those who cannot afford to fail.

Bone started with a gap no one was closing: AI software was accelerating, but the physical systems a nation depends on — defense, disaster response, critical infrastructure — were still built from disconnected parts. We started Bone to build them as one system, and to stay in the field until it works. Every system we ship carries the urgency, precision, and purpose of the team that built it — mission-ready, adaptive under pressure, built for the edge of contested environments.
