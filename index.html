The Thesis

Bone builds and deploys physical AI systems that safeguard the time, freedom, and security upon which modern civilization depends.

Intelligent machines don't fail because of bad algorithms. They fail because the infrastructure underneath them doesn't exist.

Every autonomous system depends on a stack: software to decide, hardware to act, communications to connect, and manufacturing to produce at scale. Today that stack is fragmented across vendors who don't talk to each other.

Bone builds the entire stack as one system. The result is autonomy that actually works when it matters — in a wildfire, on a contested border, at national scale.
