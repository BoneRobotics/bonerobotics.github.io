PRIVACY
POLICY

Effective: January 2025 · Bone Robotics Inc.

Bone Robotics is built privacy-first. Our website sets no cookies, runs no third-party analytics, and embeds no advertising or behavioural trackers. This policy explains the limited data we handle and the choices you have.

At a glance

No cookies. No trackers. No analytics. We only process the information you choose to send us — for example, when you contact us — and we use it solely to respond to you.

01 Who we are

Bone Robotics Inc. (“Bone Robotics”, “we”, “us”) is a Physical AI and autonomous systems company headquartered in Seoul, Republic of Korea. We are the data controller responsible for the personal information described in this policy. For any privacy question, contact us at info@bone.ai.

02 Information we collect

We keep collection to a minimum.

  • Information you provide. When you reach out through our contact channels, we receive what you choose to share — typically your name, email address, organization, and the contents of your message.
  • Basic server logs. Like most websites, our hosting infrastructure may briefly record technical request data (such as IP address, timestamp, and requested page) for security and reliability. These logs are not used to profile or track you.
  • No cookies or analytics. We do not place cookies, use analytics suites, fingerprinting, or advertising technologies on this site.

03 Third-party services

This site loads typography from Google Fonts. When the fonts load, your browser makes a request to Google’s servers, which necessarily reveals your IP address to Google. We do not share any other information with Google, and we use no other third-party scripts. If you submit an inquiry, your message may be handled through our email provider strictly to deliver and store correspondence.

04 How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and communicate with you.
  • To evaluate and progress partnership, procurement, or recruitment discussions you initiate.
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and availability of our site.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising or automated profiling.

05 Legal bases

Where applicable law requires a legal basis, we rely on your consent (when you contact us), our legitimate interests in operating and securing our site and responding to legitimate business inquiries, and compliance with legal obligations.

06 Sharing & disclosure

We share personal information only with a small number of service providers that help us operate (for example, hosting and email), under appropriate confidentiality obligations; where required by law or to respond to lawful requests by public authorities; and in connection with a corporate transaction such as a merger or acquisition. We do not otherwise disclose your information to third parties.

07 Retention

We retain inquiry-related information only as long as needed to handle your request and for a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping, then delete or anonymize it. Server logs are kept for a short period and rotated.

08 Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under Korea’s PIPA and the EU/UK GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal information, to object to processing, and to data portability. To exercise any of these rights, email info@bone.ai. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

09 Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the limited information we hold. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data and to limit what we collect in the first place.

10 International transfers

We are based in the Republic of Korea. If you contact us from another country, your information will be processed in Korea and, where our service providers operate, potentially elsewhere. We take steps to ensure such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards.

11 Children

Our site and services are intended for businesses and government organizations and are not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above and, where appropriate, by additional notice.

13 Contact

Questions about this policy or your personal information? Email info@bone.ai or reach us through our contact page.
