Privacy policy.

How Bone handles information. We keep it simple: we don't track you.

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Bone AI Inc. ("Bone," "we," "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle personal information when you visit this site or contact us. We process personal data in line with the Republic of Korea's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA) and, where it applies, the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

01ControllerThe data controller is Bone AI Inc. (Business Registration No. 727-88-03866), 13th Floor, 103-ho, 518, Teheran-ro, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, Republic of Korea. For any privacy question or request, reach us at info@bonerobotics.ai.
02No trackingThis site uses no cookies, no third-party trackers, and no analytics. We do not profile visitors, serve advertising, or sell or share data with advertisers. Because we set no cookies, no cookie consent is needed to browse.
03Information we collectWe only collect what you choose to send us — typically when you contact us for procurement, partnership, investment, press, or careers. That generally includes your name, organization, email address, phone number (if you provide it), and the contents of your message. Providing it is voluntary, but without it we may be unable to respond. We do not knowingly collect sensitive data or information from children.
04Why we use it, and our legal basisWe use your information solely to respond to your inquiry and to assess a potential engagement. Under the GDPR, our legal basis is our legitimate interest in handling business inquiries and taking steps at your request before any contract (Art. 6(1)(b) and (f)), or your consent where we ask for it (Art. 6(1)(a)). Under PIPA, we process this information to respond to the request you submit. We do not use it for any unrelated purpose, and we do not carry out automated decision-making or profiling.
05Sharing and processorsWe do not sell your information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate (for example, email or hosting), who are bound to protect it and use it only on our instructions; where required to comply with the law or a lawful request; or to protect the security of our systems, people, and operations.
06International transfersWe are based in Seoul and also operate in the United States, so your information may be stored in or accessed from countries outside your own. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we rely on a lawful mechanism — for transfers from the EEA/UK to the Republic of Korea, the European Commission's adequacy decision for Korea; for other transfers, Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards.
07Retention and destructionWe keep inquiry information only as long as needed for the purpose it was provided, unless a longer period is required by law. When it is no longer needed, we destroy it without delay — deleting electronic files irrecoverably and shredding any physical records.
08Your rightsSubject to applicable law, you may ask us to access, correct, delete, or export your information, to restrict or object to its processing, or to suspend processing; where processing relies on consent, you may withdraw it at any time. To exercise a right, contact us at info@bonerobotics.ai. We verify your identity first and respond within the period set by applicable law.
09SecurityWe apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the data — including access controls, limiting access to authorized personnel, and secure handling — to protect personal information against loss, misuse, or unauthorized access.
10ComplaintsYou may contact us with any concern, and you also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority — in Korea, the Personal Information Protection Commission (pipc.go.kr); in the EEA/UK, your local authority.
11ChangesWe may update this policy as our practices or legal obligations change. The current version and its effective date are always shown at the top of this page.
12ContactQuestions about this policy can be directed to info@bonerobotics.ai. Bone AI Inc. is based in Seoul, Republic of Korea.
